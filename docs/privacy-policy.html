Icon Pack Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all of our icon pack applications (each referred to here as "the app", and collectively "our apps"). Our icon packs provide themed launcher icons that you can apply through a supported third-party launcher. We respect your privacy: our apps do not require an account, and they do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to us or to any third party.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or transmit any personal data. Our apps have no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking. Nothing you do in an app is sent to us, because our apps do not operate any backend service for data collection.

3. On-Device Processing

To provide icon-pack features, an app may read the list of launchable applications and supported launcher packages installed on your device. This is used only to display compatible apps, an icon gallery, and launcher setup guidance. This processing happens entirely on your device and is never transmitted off the device or shared with us.

4. Permissions

Where required by the operating system, an app may request permission to query installed applications so it can show which apps an icon is available for. This permission is used solely for on-device icon-pack functionality and for no other purpose.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data, because we do not collect any. Our apps do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker SDKs.

6. Trademarks

All product names, logos, and brands referenced by the icons are the property of their respective owners and are used only to indicate the applications the icons are designed for. Our apps are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by these companies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13 and do not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change, we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at theappbuilder.labs@gmail.com.

This policy applies to all of our published icon pack applications.
