Icon Pack Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all of our icon pack applications (each referred to here as "the app", and collectively "our apps"). Our icon packs provide themed launcher icons that you can apply through a supported third-party launcher. Our apps do not require an account. Core icon-pack features run entirely on your device; some apps also include an optional feature that lets you request new icons, which transmits limited data only when you choose to use it (see Section 3).

2. Information We Collect Automatically

We do not collect any personal data automatically. Our apps have no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no background tracking. Simply browsing icons and applying the pack sends nothing to us.

3. Data We Collect When You Submit a Request

Some of our apps include an optional feature to request new icons. It only sends data when you actively select apps and submit a request. When you do, we may collect:

  • The package names and labels of the apps you selected
  • App icon URI values, if provided by your device
  • Your current launcher details (label, package name, activity name)
  • A submission timestamp and which icon pack the request came from
  • A random device identifier generated by the app (used for rate limiting)

We do not transmit the full list of installed apps on your device. Only the apps and details you choose to submit through the request feature are sent.

4. On-Device Processing & Permissions

To provide icon-pack features, an app may read the list of launchable applications and supported launcher packages installed on your device. This is used only to display compatible apps, an icon gallery, launcher setup guidance, and the app-selection screen for the optional request feature. This processing happens on your device; nothing leaves the device unless you submit a request as described in Section 3.

5. How We Use Data

  • Process the icon requests you submit and prioritize future icon updates
  • Detect abuse and apply rate limiting to submissions
  • Improve icon quality and compatibility across launchers

6. Data Sharing & Storage

We do not sell or rent personal data. Request data you submit is transmitted to and stored on our backend infrastructure provider (Supabase) solely to operate the service. Our apps do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker SDKs.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Request records are retained as needed for product improvement, quality analysis, and abuse prevention.

How to request deletion: Email theappbuilder.labs@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion", from the device you submitted from or including your app-generated device identifier. We will delete the records associated with you — the selected app names and labels, launcher details, submission timestamps, and your device identifier — within 30 days of the request. Aggregated or anonymized data that no longer identifies you or your device may be retained.

8. Trademarks

All product names, logos, and brands referenced by the icons are the property of their respective owners and are used only to indicate the applications the icons are designed for. Our apps are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by these companies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

10. Contact

For any questions about this policy or to request data deletion, contact us at theappbuilder.labs@gmail.com.

If our data practices change, we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date above. This policy applies to all of our published icon pack applications.
