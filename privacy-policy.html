Fluffy Icon Pack Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Fluffy Icon Pack ("we", "our", "us") provides launcher icon pack functionality and an optional feedback feature for icon requests and mismatch reports.

2. On-Device Processing

To provide icon-pack features, the app may inspect launchable apps installed on your device and check for supported launcher packages. This is used to show compatible apps, launcher setup options, and app-selection UI for optional feedback. This processing occurs on your device.

The app may also store limited data in the app's internal local storage, such as a random feedback device identifier and lists of apps you have already submitted through the feedback flow, to support rate limiting and reduce duplicate submissions.

3. Data We Collect When You Submit Feedback

When you explicitly submit feedback from within the app, we may collect:

  • Feedback type (icon request or mismatch report)
  • Selected app package names
  • Selected app labels and icon URI values (if provided by your device)
  • Current launcher details (label, package name, activity name)
  • Submission timestamp
  • A random feedback device identifier generated by the app

We do not automatically transmit the full list of installed apps on your device to our servers. Only the apps and related details you choose to submit through the feedback feature are transmitted.

4. How We Use Data

  • Show icon-pack compatibility features and launcher guidance within the app
  • Process icon requests and mismatch reports
  • Prioritize future icon updates
  • Detect abuse and apply rate limiting to feedback submissions
  • Improve icon quality and compatibility across launchers

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Feedback data is processed through our backend infrastructure providers only to operate the service.

6. Data Retention

Feedback records are retained as needed for product improvement, quality analysis, and abuse prevention, and removed when no longer required.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Your Choices

Feedback submission is optional. You may choose not to submit feedback. For deletion or privacy requests, contact us through our official app listing support channel.

10. Contact

Support contact details are provided on the app store listing.

If our data practices change, we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date.
